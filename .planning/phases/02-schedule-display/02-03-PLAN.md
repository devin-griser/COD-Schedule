     - `const completedGroups = groupByDate(completed, timeZone, getDateKey, formatDateLabel)`

    - Header wrapper: `<header className="mb-8">`
  - **Upcoming section:** `<MatchSection title="Upcoming" groups={upcomingGroups} teamMap={teamMap} timeZone={timeZone} />`
  - **Results section:** `<MatchSection title="Results" groups={completedGroups} teamMap={teamMap} timeZone={timeZone} />`
